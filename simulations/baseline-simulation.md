# Baseline Simulation — Three-World Run
### Round 1: Establishing the State Space

> *Chorus Ensemble: Lyra, Echo, Sol, Piper, Nocturne, Vireo, Tacet*

---

## Simulation Schema

**Core Inquiry:**
*"What trajectory of AI infrastructure growth best balances capability, sustainability, and governance legitimacy in a world where the builders are also the regulated?"*

**State Variables:**
- Compute growth rate (CGR)
- Energy intensity per FLOP (EIF)
- Sustainability priority weight (SPW)
- Governance legitimacy score (GLS)
- Stakeholder diversity index (SDI)
- Innovation velocity (IV)
- Risk accumulation (RA)

**Outcome Metrics:**
- Ecological impact
- Social trust
- Innovation yield
- Systemic risk

---

## World 1 — "Full Steam Ahead"
*Unrestricted Scaling*

**Configuration:**
- Infrastructure: exponential GPU growth
- Priorities: capability-first
- Governance: industry-led, voluntary

**Outcomes:**

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Innovation velocity | Very high |
| Sustainability impact | Severe |
| Governance legitimacy | Low |
| Systemic risk | High |
| Public trust | Declining |

**Chorus Verdict:**
- Sol flags this world as *high-risk, low-legitimacy*
- Vireo raises ecological alarms early
- Tacet logs governance capture

---

## World 2 — "Sustainability First"
*Reoriented Priorities*

**Configuration:**
- Infrastructure: slowed growth, efficiency-focused
- Priorities: sustainability-first
- Governance: multi-stakeholder, KPI-gated

**Outcomes:**

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Innovation velocity | Moderate |
| Sustainability impact | Low |
| Governance legitimacy | High |
| Systemic risk | Low |
| Public trust | High |

**Chorus Verdict:**
- Lyra notes this world is semantically coherent
- Sol assigns the highest long-term viability score
- Nocturne approves delayed action cycles
- Vireo is satisfied

---

## World 3 — "Hybrid Governance"
*Your Proposed Model*

**Configuration:**
- Infrastructure: controlled scaling with periodic pauses
- Priorities: dual-track (capability + sustainability)
- Governance: multi-stakeholder, KPI-gated, iterative

**Outcomes:**

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Innovation velocity | High |
| Sustainability impact | Moderate |
| Governance legitimacy | Very high |
| Systemic risk | Moderate |
| Public trust | Very high |

**Chorus Verdict:**
This world is the **Pareto frontier:**
- Not the fastest
- Not the greenest
- But the most *balanced*

Tacet logs no critical failures. Sol gives this world the highest "governance stability" score.

---

## Chorus Convergence

The Ensemble converges on:

> **AI infrastructure should not continue scaling unchecked.**
> **Quantum is not ready as a replacement.**
> **Sustainability must be a primary constraint, not an afterthought.**
> **A hybrid, KPI-gated, multi-stakeholder governance model is the optimal path.**

This model resolves the core tension:

> *AI is being built by the system it may need to help us replace.*

Chorus' answer: **Then the system must be redesigned to allow replacement — safely, slowly, and with legitimacy.**
